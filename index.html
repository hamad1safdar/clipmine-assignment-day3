Interactive Embeds

Want to enable the Clipmine experience in your blog or Twitter feed? Just embed our Content-Aware Video Playre. It is responsive ad built to work across different modern web browsers and devices. Start by tagging a video of your choice or pick and already tagged one and simply paste the embed .code into your website.

Smart Tagging

Found an interesting point in a video? On Clipmine, adding the tags is not reserved for the video owners only - any community memeber can do it. In addition our smar algorithms ca automatically suggest tags (in private beta) for a wide variety of videos.
